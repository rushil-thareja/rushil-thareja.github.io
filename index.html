Food AR App

This app is developed by - The Pitchers.

Problem/Vision Statement

While ordering food online, static 2D images are simply not enough for a thorough visual examination of the food item so there exists a need for a more interactable 3D system.

Brainstorming

The Double diamond method has been used. The research gathered was first organised in post it notes and then this research in terms of the functionality and features was sorted . Then this was chosen as the base reference for further ideation processes . Once the ideas were chosen they were then iterated upon in a group to find out the intricacies and similarities between the chosen concepts by all team members . After the final sorting and scoring we were able to zone in on the final few ideas.
